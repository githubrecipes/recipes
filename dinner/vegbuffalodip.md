## Directions
(@) Combine in bowl
    - Sour Cream
    - Cream Cheese
    - Ranch Dressing Mix
    Until smooth
(@) Stir in
    - Cheese
    - Black beans
    - Mushrooms
    - Buffalo Wing Sauce
(@) Transfer to crockpot
(@) Cook for **1.5hr**
(@) Serve with lime tortilla chips
