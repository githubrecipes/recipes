## Directions
(@) Mash
    - Beef
    - Baking soda
(@) Let sit **10-15 min** to tenderize
(@) Preheat oven to **350F**
(@) Heat
    - Butter
    Until Bubbling
(@) Add
    - Onion
    - Bell Pepper
    - Garlic
(@) Cook (while **stirring**) for **4-6 min**
(@) Add
    - Beef
    - Salt
    - Pepper
    - Paprika
    - Cumin
    - Oregano
    - Mustard
(@) Cook
    Until mostly brown (**~3 min**)
(@) Add
    - Tomato Sauce
    - Tomato Paste
    - Worcestershire Sauce
    - hot sauce (to taste)
(@) Reduce heat
(@) Simmer for **10 min**
    Until sauce has thickened
