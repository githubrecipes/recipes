## Directions
(@) Cook chicken in olive oil
    Until no longer pink
(@) Drain
(@) Stir in
    - Wing Sauce
    - Spaghetti Sauce
(@) Mix in small bowl
    - Ricotta
    - Mozzarella Cheese
(@) Spread Sauce Mixture on bottom of crockpot
(@) Layer until depleted:
    - 3 noodles
    - 1 cup sauce
    - Peppers
    - cheese mix
(@) Cook on **low** for **4-5 hours**
