## Directions
(@) Combine in slow cooker:
    - Onion
    - Bell Pepper
    - Pinto Beans
    - Kidney Beans
    - Crushed Tomatoes
    - Chili Powder
    - Cumin
    - Salt
    - Black Pepper
    - Veg Broth
(@) Cover and cook on **high** for **4** hours or **low** for **6-8** hours
(@) Stir in pasta
    - Cook **15-20**
    - Or until pasta cooked through
(@) Stir in cheese
