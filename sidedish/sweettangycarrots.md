## Directions

(@) Place 1 in. of water and carrots sliced in large saucepan
(@) Bring to a boil
(@) Cover and simmer for **15-20** min
(@) Drain
(@) Return carrots to saucepan
(@) Add
    - Brown sugar
    - Butter
    - Dijon Mustard
    - Pepper
(@) Cook and stir over low heat until well coated.
